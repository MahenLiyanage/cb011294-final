Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery


The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is known for sea turtle protection. The hatchery maintains these amazing species with its long history of turtle protection.They guard sea turtle eggs and hatchlings. The hatchery incubates eggs from bird-nesting coasts. Baby turtles are nurtured in tanks until they can survive in the ocean.The Hatchery promotes sea turtle conservation and preserves hatchlings. Turtle species, life cycles, and challenges are taught. The hatchery educates and protects turtles. The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery's dedication has saved Sri Lankan marine turtles. It enhanced turtle populations, safeguarded nesting habitats, and promoted awareness of the necessity of protecting these gorgeous creatures. The hatchery inspires tourists and turtle conservation.Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is unique and educational. Visitors can engage with hatchlings, see conservation efforts, and release them into the ocean. Explore nature, learn about sea turtles, and help them. The community works to preserve these ancient mariners at the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery.

Other Activities


kosgoda turtle conservation centre

The Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Centre in Sri Lanka is known for protecting marine turtles. The centre protects turtle nests, incubates eggs, and releases hatchlings into the ocean. Sea turtle behaviour and ecology studies is also essential. The centre promotes turtle conservation and encourages local communities to protect these amazing species. The Research Centre helps sea turtles survive by supporting sustainable practises.

Kosgoda Spice Gardens

The picturesque Kosgoda Spice Gardens in Sri Lanka is recognised for its wide variety of fragrant spices. The gardens immerse visitors in the lively world of spices and their cultivation, processing, and traditional usage. Visitors can see cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, and nutmeg growing and learn about their use in Sri Lankan food and medicine. Kosgoda Spice Gardens are also perfect for cultural exchange and buying high-quality spices and herbs. It's a delightful spot to enjoy spices and Sri Lankan culture.

Kosgoda Beach

Sri Lanka's Kosgoda Beach is picturesque. Visitors come to this beach stretch for its tranquilly and beauty. The beach is popular for swimming and sunbathing due to its calm seas. The beach's abundant foliage and palm trees will please nature lovers. The turtle hatchery at Kosgoda Beach lets tourists watch sea turtle hatchlings being released into the ocean. Kosgoda Beach offers a relaxing coastal vacation with breathtaking views and unique animals.
